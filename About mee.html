About Me - Dom's Sugarland

Hi there! I'm Dominion.

I'm a college student studying Computer Information Systems with a passion for cooking. Originally from Nigeria, I've always been deeply connected to my culture and traditions, especially through food.

Bringing Nigerian Cuisine to the World

My love for cooking inspired me to open Funmi's Kitchen, a restaurant where I bring the vibrant flavors of Nigerian cuisine to the world. It's not just about food for me; it's about sharing my heritage and giving others the opportunity to experience the rich and diverse tastes of Nigeria.

Combining Passion with Studies

While pursuing my degree in Computer Information Systems, I've found ways to integrate my passion for cooking into my academic life. Whether it's developing innovative software solutions for the restaurant industry or using technology to streamline operations at Funmi's Kitchen, I'm always looking for ways to merge my interests.
